What is Physical Therapy and Why is it Important?

Physical therapy is a healthcare specialty focused on helping individuals improve mobility, reduce pain, restore function, and prevent disability. Whether you’re recovering from surgery, dealing with arthritis, managing sports injuries, or coping with chronic conditions like back pain, physical therapy offers personalized treatment plans designed to help you regain your best quality of life. A licensed physical therapist assesses your condition and designs a targeted rehabilitation program, incorporating exercises, manual therapy, education, and sometimes advanced modalities like electrical stimulation or ultrasound.
